Chai Tea

A cup of nice warm tea is always in season! Enjoy at about 2 in the afternoon when you're fading fast and need a pickmeup

Ingredients

  • chai tea bag
  • milk
  • hot water
  • cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. boil water
  2. add hot water to cup with tea bag
  3. add splash of milk
  4. garnish with cinnamon stick
  5. forget about it until your boyfriend reminds you
  6. drink lukewarm tea
