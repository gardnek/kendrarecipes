Crockpot Chili

Crockpot chili is an easy, delicious way to warm up that requires very little work

Ingredients

  • beans
  • meat
  • corn
  • chili mix
  • peppers
  • any veg you have lying around

Steps

  1. throw everything in a crockpot
  2. cook on low for 8 hours
  3. serve with cheese, hot sauce, chips
