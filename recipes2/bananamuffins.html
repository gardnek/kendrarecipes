Banana Bread Muffins

The best way to use up those bananas that you got in your veggie box that you'll never eat

Ingredients

  • 4 overripe bananas
  • brown sugar
  • melted butter
  • chocolate chips
  • flour

Steps

  1. Mash bananas with a fork
  2. add 2 cups of sugar
  3. add melted butter
  4. add eggs & mix
  5. add flour and mix together
  6. spoon into muffin tins
  7. bake for 25 minutes
  8. eat!
